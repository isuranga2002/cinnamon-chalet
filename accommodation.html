Your Home Away From Home

The Villa

Cinnamon Chalet offers a spacious room with a comfortable large bed, air conditioning, and mosquito net. The room is perfect for couples or families with up to 2 children, with an extra bed available upon request.

The villa features a fully equipped kitchen and an English-style bathroom. Guests can relax on a large comfortable sofa bed and futon in the cozy living room area.

Enjoy our reading terrace with panoramic views and peaceful nature sounds. Many guests mention that they hear only the sounds of birds during their stay.

The entire home is available for reservation. Hosts do not stay with guests during an entire home booking, but are happy to meet guests whenever they wish.

Authentic Sri Lankan Cuisine

Home-Cooked Meals

Experience amazing Sri Lankan meals prepared with care and attention. Our meals can be customized based on your preferences and dietary requirements.

Guests are welcome to join our cooking sessions and learn the art of traditional Sri Lankan food preparation. It's a wonderful way to take a piece of Sri Lanka home with you.

The fully equipped kitchen is available for guests who prefer to cook their own meals.

We source fresh vegetables and herbal green leaves from local village farmers, supporting the community while ensuring the freshest ingredients for your meals.
