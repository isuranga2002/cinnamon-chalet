Things To Do

Unforgettable Moments

From peaceful sunrise views to exciting cultural adventures, discover the best of Sri Lanka.

Sunrise at Your Doorstep

Begin your day with breathtaking sunrise views right from the villa. Watch as the golden light illuminates the paddy fields below while birds take flight over the cinnamon mountains.

Peacocks can often be spotted gracefully walking through the paddy fields. This tranquil morning ritual is one of the most cherished experiences our guests remember.

Cinnamon Mountain Walk

Explore the famous cinnamon plantations that give our chalet its name. Take a guided walk through the scenic mountains and learn about the traditional cinnamon harvesting process.

The fresh mountain air and lush greenery make this an invigorating experience for nature lovers.

Cultural Day Trips

Discover the rich culture of the region with arranged city day trips. Visit the famous moonstone village where skilled artisans craft beautiful jewelry, and explore the traditional mask museum showcasing Sri Lanka's vibrant artistic heritage.

Other popular stops include the turtle hatchery, where you can see conservation efforts firsthand, and ancient temples that tell stories of centuries past.

Galle Fort & Beach Tour

Embark on a memorable jungle trip and historical tour of the iconic Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Explore the charming colonial streets, boutique shops, and stunning ocean views.

On the way, stop for a refreshing swim at the beautiful beaches of Ambalangoda and Hikkaduwa, known for their golden sands and crystal-clear waters.

Cooking Classes

Join us in the kitchen and learn the secrets of authentic Sri Lankan cuisine. Our cooking sessions are hands-on experiences where you'll prepare traditional dishes using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients.

Take the flavors of Sri Lanka home with you as you master recipes passed down through generations.
