Our Story

Welcome to Cinnamon Chalet

Cinnamon Chalet is more than just a place to stayâ€”it's a family home that we open to guests from around the world. Nestled in the peaceful countryside of Ambalangoda, Sri Lanka, our villa offers a retreat from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

Named after the fragrant cinnamon mountains that surround us, our chalet has been thoughtfully designed to provide comfort while preserving the authentic charm of Sri Lankan living.

We believe in sustainable tourism and actively support our local community. Fresh vegetables and herbs are sourced from village farmers, ensuring you enjoy the freshest ingredients while contributing to local livelihoods.

When you stay with us, you become part of our extended family. We take pride in sharing our culture, cuisine, and the natural beauty of our homeland with every guest.
